    The Practice Map

    The Practice Map helps senior technology practitioners classify stuck situations, choose an evidence and governance posture, and produce a defensible next-step frame. One map underneath (eleven composable situation patterns); four working faces on top. Internally the role it describes is nicknamed "the Conductor" — a shorthand, not a market label.

    How to use. First time → Introduction. Starting a subject → Face 1: run the Classify wizard (it generates your one-page situation frame). Asked what you do → Say it. Losing a room → Hold the room. Hiring or explaining the career → Transmit & project.
