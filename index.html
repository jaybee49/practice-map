The Conductor — a practice map

A field instrument for a role this document names the Conductor — a proposed name for a new category, not a market label: the practitioner who takes stuck, high-stakes technology situations — a decision, a deal, a build, a dispute, an acquisition — and conducts them to outcomes their owners can defend. One map underneath; four faces on top.

How to use. First time here → Introduction, then the five operations. Starting a subject → Enter. Asked what you do → Say it. Losing a room → Hold the room. Hiring, training, or explaining the career → Transmit & project.
